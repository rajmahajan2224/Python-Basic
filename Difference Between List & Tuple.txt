Difference between List & Tuple -

List:-
(i) List are Mutable.
(ii) List iterations time is more.
(iii) Unexpected chnages and error more likely to occur.
(iv) Consume more memory.
(v) Have many bulit-In Types.

Tuple:-
(i) Tuple are Immutable.
(ii) Tuple iterations time is less.
(iii) Unexpected changes and error are hard to occur.
(iv) Consume less memory in comparison to list.
(v) Have Few Built-In Types.
